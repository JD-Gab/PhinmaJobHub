About Us

PHINMA Job Hub is a website dedicated for the alumni of PHINMA UI and the university's partners for applicants and employers, respectively.

PHINMA Job Hub for Alumni

PHINMA Job Hub is made for the alumni of PHINMA University of Iloilo.

As graduate students take part in the society, job searching is a way to apply and show their skills and experience they gained from their education in PHINMA UI. This website is created for their convenience in order to easily hunt jobs and apply to particular jobs that is aligned with their interest.

PHINMA Job Hub Employers

PHINMA Job Hub's main employers are the university's company partners.

As PHINMA Job Hub's users is dedicated for alumni of PHINMA UI, the employers are also made as the major employer of the website. From companies that hires criminology students, or companies that are looking for IT graduates, the alumni of PHINMA UI can easily find jobs as easily as the companies can post theirs.
